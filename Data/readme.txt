eventsVLC.csv: file with all relevant events in the city of Valencia (Spain) between 01/07/2019-23-03/2020.
hotelsVLC.csv: file with all the hotels (from 1 to 5 star) in the city of Valencia (Spain) published by Booking.com.
URLevents.txt: URL events used to get relevant events in the city.
prices folder: csv files with hotel room prices scraped from Booking.com site.
